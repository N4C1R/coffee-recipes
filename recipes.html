Coffee Recipes

Master the art of coffee brewing with our comprehensive collection of professional recipes

Classic Espresso

The foundation of all coffee drinks

Dose: 18-20g
Yield: 36-40g
Time: 25-30s
Temp: 94°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Grind 18-20g of coffee to fine consistency
  2. Dose and distribute evenly in portafilter
  3. Tamp with 30lbs of pressure
  4. Extract for 25-30 seconds
  5. Stop at 36-40g yield

Ristretto

Concentrated espresso shot

Dose: 18-20g
Yield: 18-20g
Time: 15-20s
Temp: 94°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Use same dose as regular espresso
  2. Grind slightly finer than espresso
  3. Stop extraction at half the volume
  4. Aim for 15-20 second extraction
  5. Result: Intense, syrupy shot

Cappuccino

Perfect balance of espresso and milk

Espresso: 1 shot
Milk: 150ml
Time: 2-3min
Temp: 65°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Pull a single shot of espresso
  2. Steam 150ml milk to 65°C
  3. Create thick microfoam
  4. Pour milk over espresso
  5. Top with 2cm of foam

Latte

Smooth and creamy milk coffee

Espresso: 1-2 shots
Milk: 200-250ml
Time: 2-3min
Temp: 65°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Pull 1-2 shots of espresso
  2. Steam milk to silky microfoam
  3. Start with pitcher high
  4. Lower for latte art
  5. Finish with thin foam layer

Flat White

Silky microfoam perfection

Espresso: 2 shots
Milk: 120ml
Time: 2-3min
Temp: 60°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Pull double shot of espresso
  2. Steam small amount of milk
  3. Create very thin microfoam
  4. Pour smoothly and quickly
  5. No thick foam layer

Pour Over (V60)

Clean and bright extraction

Coffee: 20g
Water: 300g
Time: 3-4min
Temp: 96°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Rinse filter and preheat dripper
  2. Add 20g medium-fine ground coffee
  3. Bloom with 40g water for 30s
  4. Pour in circular motions to 300g
  5. Total brew time: 3-4 minutes

French Press

Full-bodied immersion brewing

Coffee: 30g
Water: 500g
Time: 4min
Temp: 93°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Add 30g coarse ground coffee
  2. Pour 500g hot water (93°C)
  3. Stir gently and place lid
  4. Steep for 4 minutes
  5. Press plunger down slowly

AeroPress

Versatile pressure brewing

Coffee: 18g
Water: 250g
Time: 2min
Temp: 85°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Add 18g medium-fine ground coffee
  2. Pour 250g water (85°C)
  3. Stir for 10 seconds
  4. Steep for 1 minute
  5. Press gently for 30 seconds

Cold Brew

Smooth, low-acid coffee concentrate

Coffee: 100g
Water: 1000g
Time: 12-24h
Temp: Room Temp

Brewing Steps:

  1. Coarsely grind 100g coffee beans
  2. Mix with 1000g cold water
  3. Steep for 12-24 hours
  4. Filter through fine mesh
  5. Dilute concentrate 1:1 with water

Turkish Coffee

Traditional unfiltered brewing

Coffee: 10g
Water: 100g
Time: 3-4min
Temp: 100°C

Brewing Steps:

  1. Add 10g extra fine coffee to cezve
  2. Add 100g cold water and sugar to taste
  3. Stir well and heat slowly
  4. Remove foam when it forms
  5. Pour into cup with grounds
