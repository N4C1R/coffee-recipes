Time Optimization Tips

  • Grind finer to increase extraction time
  • Grind coarser to decrease extraction time
  • Longer time = more extraction (risk of bitterness)
  • Shorter time = less extraction (risk of sourness)

Troubleshooting Guide

Fix common coffee brewing problems

Bitter Taste

Possible Causes:

  • Over-extraction
  • Water too hot
  • Grind too fine
  • Brew time too long

Solutions:

  • Use coarser grind
  • Lower water temperature
  • Reduce brew time
  • Use less coffee

Sour Taste

Possible Causes:

  • Under-extraction
  • Water too cool
  • Grind too coarse
  • Brew time too short

Solutions:

  • Use finer grind
  • Increase water temperature
  • Extend brew time
  • Use more coffee

Weak Coffee

Possible Causes:

  • Too much water
  • Not enough coffee
  • Grind too coarse
  • Water too cool

Solutions:

  • Use stronger ratio (1:12)
  • Increase coffee dose
  • Use finer grind
  • Check water temperature

Clogging

Possible Causes:

  • Grind too fine
  • Too much coffee
  • Poor quality grinder
  • Stale coffee

Solutions:

  • Use coarser grind
  • Reduce coffee dose
  • Invest in burr grinder
  • Use fresh coffee
